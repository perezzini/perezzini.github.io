**SocialIcon** — a bare Font Awesome glyph link for the footer social row; gray at rest, navy on hover, no underline.

```jsx
<SocialIcon icon="fa-brands fa-linkedin-in" href="https://linkedin.com/in/perezzini" label="LinkedIn" />
<SocialIcon icon="fa-brands fa-github" href="https://github.com/perezzini" label="GitHub" />
```

- Lay out a row with `display:flex; gap:20px`. Requires Font Awesome 6 loaded.
