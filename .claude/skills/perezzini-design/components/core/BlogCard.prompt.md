**BlogCard** — an essay preview: small gray date, navy Prata title link (underlines on hover), gray description. Left-aligned, no border or fill — it sits directly on the page.

```jsx
<BlogCard
  date="May 31, 2026"
  title="Hello, World"
  description="The first post on this site — a brief note on why I'm starting to write."
  href="/essays/hello-world"
/>
```

- Stack several with their built-in 24px bottom margin.
